My Favorite Hobby: Reading Books

My favorite hobby is reading books. I enjoy reading because I love learning new stories and random intresting facts. For example, did you know Dhugal Lindsay is a marine researcher who discovered a new type of jellyfish called the Lantern Jellyfish? He also does poetry! My favorite genre is adventure-fiction, or just fiction in general. Though I do read other genres as well. Such as; poetry books, historical fictions, mystery, Greek and Roman mythological fiction, children's litterture, and much more. At 9pm every night, my family and I sit at the dinner table and read for 30 minutes. I also like to go on Webtoon and read online during my free time (or watch because webtoon consists of comics). Before the pandemic hit I used to go the libaray at my school and ask the librarian for recommendations. I believe that you can learn at least one thing from every book and so you can gain more knowledge while still reading for fun.


My Favorite Books


My favorite book is "The Extremely Inconvenient Adventures of Bronte Mettlestone by Jaclyn Moriarty, rated 4.3/5 stars. It's an adventure/fantasy fiction book for ages ranging from 10-14 year olds. The book is about a 10 year old girl, Bronte Mettlestone, who lost her parents due to pirates and is tasked to deliver 10 gifts to each of her aunts (all which live in different kingdoms) according to their will. And she must do it alone.


What I like about Bronte is that she's very smart and polite. Throughout her travels she shows an amazing display of knowledge and manners. She grew up with her Aunt Isabella who is the eldest and most mannered aunt. Each aunt Bronte visits gives her a new experience and near the end of the book, she discovers a secret.

My favorite book series is "Peter Nimble". The series consists of 2 books. The first, "Peter Nimble and his Fantastic Eyes", and the second, "Sophie Quire and the Last Storyguard." The series is a fantasy fiction genre with a 4.1 star rating and the second book with a 4.4 rating writen by Jonathan Auxier.


When I first read the first book I was amazed by all the details and the intresting characters that showed up. Even with almost no illustrations I can still picture the images in my head. The second book had a plot that I've always dreamed of being in (spoiler alert). Ever since I was little I would imagine hopping into differnt stories and this book brought me the closest to experiencing that!
